1    # Classwize Classroom Setup
2    
3    The College uses Family Zone/Linewize web filtering to monitor and filter our internet traffic. For
4    students, YouTube is blocked by default, as are other media intensive websites/services to prevent
5    our bandwidth being stretched to the limit. There are some industrious students who manage to find
6    workarounds from time to time, such as hot-spotting to their phone. At other times a specific word
7    on a page may prompt a block that you’ll need to open up.
8    If a website is blocked by the school (not by the DET – as some are), you can provide timed student
9    access, through the Classwize interface, but you’ll need to have Classes set up.
10   
11   > **DET Blocking vs School Blocking**
12   >
13   > If a website is blocked by the school (not by the DET – as some are), you can provide timed student
14   access, through the Classwize interface, but you’ll need to have Classes set up.
15   >

18   ## Creating A Class
19   
20   1. Access the website: [Classwize](https://classroom.au-1.familyzone.io/login/UCFSuV1Zt_)
21      >You can also find Classwize in the “STAR” favourites menu in Compass.
22   2. Select “Yarrawonga College” (not the one with .vic.au)
23      ![](Picture2.png)
24   3. Login with your T0 Number and your Network Password
25      ![](Picture3.png)
26   4. Select the “Add Class” Button in the upper right corner of the page
27      ![](Screenshot2023-06-20092231.png)
28   5. Create a name for your class – This is global across the school and each name can only be
29      used once. Please name your class with the Year Level, Subject and Teacher Code to make
30      them unique to you. (eg Year 8 Maths – BAJ)
31   6. Leave the Switch for the “Invitation Code” off. You are able to manually add students to your
32      class, which is recommended, just like in Google Classroom. Using the invitation code means
33      students need to log into the Classwize system to join a class
34   7. Select “Save”.
35   
36   ## Adding Students
37   
38   1. Click the cog icon in the lower right corner of the class on the main menu
39      ![](Screenshot2023-06-20095341.png)
40   2. Search for students via their CASES21 Code (i.e. EAL0003) or name.
41      ![](Screenshot2023-06-20100115.png)
42   3. Once all students have been added, select “Save”
43   
44   ## Adding Teachers
45   
46   1. Click the cog icon in the lower right corner of the class on the main menu
47      ![](Screenshot2023-06-20095341.png)
48   2. Navigate to the “Teachers” menu.
49   3. Search for Teachers with their T0 or name.
50      ![](Screenshot2023-06-20100435.png)
51   4. Once all teachers have been added, select “Save”.
52   
53   ## Adding Class Times
54   
55   1. Click the cog icon in the lower right corner of the class on the main menu
56      ![](Screenshot2023-06-20095341.png)
57   2. Navigate to the “Scheduling” menu.
58   3. Select “Add Schedule”.
59   4. Add all class times for this class.
60      ![](Screenshot2023-06-20102613.png)
61   5. Once all times have been entered, select “Save”.
62   > **Removing Class Times**
63   >
64   >  With scheduled classes, you’ll need to clear these out once they have ceased at the
65   end of a semester, otherwise they will continue to apply to students running in their new
66   classes.
